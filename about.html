About Us

A Premier engineering and Management Institute

BIT Mesra, located in Ranchi, India, is a premier engineering and management institute. Established in 1955, it offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various disciplines. Renowned for its academic excellence, the institute emphasizes research and innovation. It boasts a sprawling campus, state-of-the-art facilities, and a vibrant student community, making it a coveted destination for higher education in India.
