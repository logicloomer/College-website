OUR COURSES

Courses we offer

An educational Institute offering undergraduate and postgraduate courses

Undergraduate

We provide undergraduate engineering programs in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE), Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE), Mechanical Engineering, and Civil Engineering.

Postgraduate

Our postgraduate offerings include MBA, PhD, MCA and MTech programs, providing advanced education and specialized training to propel careers and foster expertise in diverse fields of study.

Our Facilities

Numerous amenities are available on campus to help students advance their knowledge.

World class Library

Our college library offers extensive resources and study materials, providing students with access to research materials and quiet study spaces conducive to academic success.

Sessional labs

Additionally, our college boasts state-of-the-art practical labs, enhancing hands-on learning experiences and enabling students to apply theoretical knowledge in real-world scenarios effectively

An expansive auditorium

Our college auditorium serves as a dynamic hub for various events, fostering collaboration and community engagement, enriching academic discourse and cultural exchange.
